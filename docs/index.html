How O3dipus became King of Thebes

The Apollo Empire, an armada of worlds ruled from planet Apollo relies on slave labor for all essential aspects of its vast economy.

While Pythia, High Priestess of Apollo, and spiritual leader of the empire hasn't left her moon temple in decades, her nets spread as wide as ever, and the schemes she devises from her sanctuary influence every destiny in the empire.

O3dipus, is an aspiring 27 year-old when this story begins.
He's just secured a position as Envoy of the Empire, which opens access to trading on empire controlled routes. ...and makes him officially a servant of the empire
