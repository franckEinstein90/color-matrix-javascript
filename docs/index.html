How O3dipus became King of Thebes

The Apollo Empire has come to rely on slave labor for all essential aspects of its vast economy.

The High Priestess of Apollo hasn't left her moon temple in decades, and her influence is diminishing across the empire.

A young and ambitious O3dipus has just been named Envoy of the Empire.

The Centaurs, a nomadic species of living spaceships, are the only practical means of interstellar transport, and therefore necessary for all trade across the empire.
By law, all Centaurs are slaves, their ownership tighly controlled by the Apollo empire

As pockets of rebellion spread across the empire, abolition, an idea that threatens Pythia and her view of the empire, is increasingly discussed.
