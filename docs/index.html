How O3dipus became King of Thebes
The Apollo Empire is in decline. Its reliance on slavery for all aspects of its vast economy, has created a static and deeply corrupt state.
While the empire's nobility enjoys immense wealth, most of the empire's citizens battle unemployement and poverty.
Soldiery is one of the few remaining available professions
a unit of semi-independent mercenaries called Envoys support and protect the slave trade
A young and ambitious O3dipus recently obtained a position as Envoy of the Empire
As part of his standard equipment kit, he's been assigned the empire centaur #12480: Akra
The Centaurs, a nomadic species of living spaceships, are the only practical means of interstellar transport, and therefore necessary for all trade across the empire.
By law, all Centaurs are slaves, their ownership tighly controlled by the Apollo empire
Pythia, the High Priestess of Apollo hasn't left her moon temple in decades.
her influence is slowly diminishing.
Pockets of rebellion are spreading across the empire. Abolition, an idea that threatens Pythia and her traditional hold on the empire, is increasingly discussed.
A group of renegade centaurs, let by the empire traitor Sphyncs has taken refuge in the Thebian system. There, they stand, a thorn in Pythia's side
